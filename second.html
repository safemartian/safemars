SafeEarth Donates $100,000 to TheOceanCleanUp Kicking Off Blockchain Eco Project

The Ocean Cleanup is a non-profit organization developing advanced technologies aiming to rid the oceans of 90% of floating plastic pollution. It aims to achieve this goal by cleaning up the Great Pacific Garbage Patch, and by catching plastic from 1000 rivers around the world to intercept 80% of the waste before it reaches the oceans.
